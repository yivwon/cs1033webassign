Favourite Products

Here are some of my favourite products that I use and recommend:

Hale Derma is a Canadian skincare brand that offers a range of products for various skin concerns. Their products are formulated with high-quality ingredients and are suitable for all skin types.
Alumine Cosmetics

Alumine Cosmetics is a Canadian brand that specializes in professional-grade skincare products. They offer a wide range of products for different skin types and concerns, including cleansers, toners, serums, and moisturizers.
Young Nails

Young Nails is a professional nail care brand that offers a wide range of products for nail technicians and nail enthusiasts. Their products include acrylic and gel systems, nail art supplies, and nail care tools.
Bio Sculpture

Bio Sculpture is a professional nail care brand that specializes in gel nail products. They offer a range of gel colors, base coats, top coats, and nail treatments that are known for their durability and shine.
Ugly Duckling Nails

Ugly Duckling Nails is a professional nail brand that offers a wide range of nail products, including gel polishes, acrylic powders, nail art supplies, and nail care tools. They are known for their high-quality products and innovative formulas.
LeChat Nails

LeChat Nails is a professional nail care brand that offers a variety of nail products, including gel polishes, dip powders, and nail art supplies. They are known for their wide range of colors and long-lasting formulas.
